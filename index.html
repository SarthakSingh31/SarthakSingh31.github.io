About Me

Hello! My name is Sarthak and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet. My interest in web development started back in 2014 when I decided to create a webpage for my school's tech department — turns out hacking together a custom fourm taught me a lot about HTML & CSS!

Fast-forward to today, and I’ve had the privilege of working at UW AXDD, a team within University of Washington dedicated towards building applications to aid students in achiving their most at the university.

While working here I've had the change of build everything from mobile apps to websites.
